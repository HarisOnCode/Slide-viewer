IV

753 BCE – 509 BCE

The Roman Kingdom

Founding myths (Romulus & Remus); Etruscan influence on early architecture, engineering, and regal insignia.

509 BCE – 27 BCE

The Roman Republic

Rise of the Senate; expansion across Italy and Mediterranean; development of the Toga as civic uniform; concrete invention.

27 BCE – 284 CE

The High Empire (Pax Romana)

Augustus to Aurelius; peak monumentalism (Colosseum, Pantheon); lavish textiles (silk trade); intricate hairstyles and jewelry.

284 CE – 476 CE

Late Antiquity & Fall

Division of East/West; shift to stiffer, ornate garments (Dalmatic); rise of Christianity influencing iconography.

Erté (Romain de Tirtoff)

The father of Art Deco fashion. Known for elongated, stylized illustrations and Harper's Bazaar covers that defined the era's glamour.

A.M. Cassandre

Revolutionized graphic design with geometric posters for railways and steamships (e.g., Nord Express), blending typography with speed.

Clarice Cliff

Industrial ceramicist famous for "Bizarre" ware. Brought bold color and angular, cubist-inspired patterns into the domestic sphere.

Émile-Jacques Ruhlmann

Premier French furniture designer. Used exotic materials like macassar ebony and ivory to create sleek, expensive forms.

The folding fan: A social instrument.

Intricate goldwork and functional ornament.

The Pearl Choker

A symbol of purity and status, worn tight against the neck, often securing a ribbon bow or cameo.

The Chatelaine

Functional jewelry worn at the waist, suspending keys, watches, and scissors—blending utility with luxury.

The Folding Fan

Essential for the "language of the fan," used to flirt, conceal, or signal social intent in the salon.

Mouches (Patches)

Small black silk patches placed on the face to hide blemishes or signal mood (e.g., near the eye for passion).

The Secular Shift

Pastoral Allegory & Play

Rococo marked a distinct shift from religious grandeur to secular intimacy. Symbolism moved towards pleasure and romance: Cupid replaced saints, seashells (rocaille) formed asymmetrical motifs, and pastoral scenes depicted idealized country life—a playful escape from court formality.

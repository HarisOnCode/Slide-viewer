Geometry of Speed

Art Deco embraced the machine age with symbols of speed, progress, and power. The sunburst represented a new dawn of modernity, the ziggurat echoed ancient power in skyscraper form, and chevrons and streamlined curves captured the velocity of flight and automotive travel.

EARLY HUMAN EXPRESSION

Era 01

OLD STONE AGE • 2.5M – 10,000 BCE

Paleolithic Period

Emergence of symbolic thought. Cave paintings at Lascaux and Chauvet depict animals and hand stencils. First use of ochre pigments and beads.

Key Artifact: Venus of Willendorf (c. 25,000 BCE)

MIDDLE STONE AGE • 10,000 – 8,000 BCE

Mesolithic Period

Transition to semi-settled life. Smaller, more detailed geometric art. Development of microlith tools and rudimentary textiles from plant fibers.

Innovation: Needle and thread technology appearing

NEW STONE AGE • 8,000 – 3,000 BCE

Neolithic Period

Agricultural revolution. Pottery production, weaving looms, and megalithic architecture (Stonehenge). Ornament becomes status.

Milestone: Woven linen and advanced dyes

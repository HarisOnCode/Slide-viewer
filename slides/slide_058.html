Illuminated Faith & Heraldry

The Medieval visual landscape was dominated by the omnipresence of the Church and feudal systems. Christian iconography in illuminated manuscripts visualized the divine through gold leaf and pigments, while heraldry emerged as a sophisticated system of colors and symbols to identify lineage, status, and allegiance on the battlefield.

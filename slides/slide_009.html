Natural materials & textures

Red Ochre (Hematite)

Symbolized life, blood, vitality, and ritual significance. Used in burials and body adornment.

Carbon Black (Charcoal)

Represented night, structure, and contrast. Primary outlining tool for cave murals.

Bone White (Kaolin/Chalk)

Symbolized spirit, bone, and purity. Often used for hand stencils and highlights.

Yellow Ochre (Limonite)

Represented earth, sun, and warmth. A foundational tone for background washes.

Women's Garments

Sheath Gowns, Bead-Nets & Royal Attire

24

01

The Kalasiris (Sheath Dress)

The quintessential linen sheath dress, form-fitting from bust to ankle held by broad straps. Often depicted in art as revealing the form beneath.

02

Bead-Net Overlays

Faience bead nets worn over plain linen shifts added color, pattern, and symbolic protection. A luxury status symbol for elite women.

03

Royal Regalia

Queens and female pharaohs like Hatshepsut adopted elements of male royal dress, including the nemes headdress and ceremonial false beard.

04

Pleating & Wigs

New Kingdom fashion introduced elaborate sun-ray pleating on fine linen robes, paired with heavy tripartite wigs and broad collars.

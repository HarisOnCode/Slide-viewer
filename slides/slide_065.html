Brooches & Fibulae

Functional fasteners evolved into status symbols. Used to pin mantles and tunics, often circular or lozenge-shaped with inscriptions.

Paternosters

Prayer beads (rosaries) worn at the waist or carried. Materials ranged from wood and bone to coral, amber, and pearls indicating piety.

Girdles & Belts

Essential for suspending purses, keys, and daggers. Elaborately decorated leather or metal plaques signified wealth and rank.

Reliquary Pendants

Hollow jewelry containing holy relics or protective text. Combined spiritual protection with personal adornment.

06. Baroque Era

Motion, Contrast &
Grandeur

Architectural Drama

Baroque design abandoned Renaissance calm for dynamic movement. Walls curved, pediments broke open, and ovals replaced static circles. Every surface became a theater for light and shadow (chiaroscuro).

Material Opulence

Textiles mirrored architecture: heavy brocades, velvets, and gold thread created physical weight and presence. Garment trims became architectural, using stiffened lace and metallic embroidery to extend the silhouette.

Animism & Magic

The Hand of the Maker

Paleolithic art was deeply rooted in animism—the belief that all things possess a spirit. Hand stencils, created by blowing pigment over a hand pressed against stone, likely served as signatures of presence, ritual connection to the earth, or hunting magic to ensure abundance.

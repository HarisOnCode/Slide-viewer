Gold (Khenet)

Symbolized divinity, the flesh of gods, and eternal life. Imperishable and radiant.

Lapis Lazuli (Khesbed)

Represented the heavens, the primeval flood, and hair of deities. A sacred blue.

Malachite Green (Wadj)

Signified growth, vegetation, resurrection, and new life. Associated with Osiris.

Red Jasper (Desher)

Represented life force, fire, anger, and victory. Associated with the desert and chaos.

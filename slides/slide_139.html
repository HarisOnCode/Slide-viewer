Primary Institutions

  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art

    Heilbrunn Timeline of Art History. Extensive collection records on Egyptian broad collars, Greek statuary, and Art Deco decorative arts.

    www.metmuseum.org
  • Victoria and Albert Museum (V&A)

    Collections: Fashion, Jewellery & Accessories. Key references for Rococo textiles, Arts & Crafts movement details, and Art Nouveau jewelry.

    www.vam.ac.uk
  • British Museum

    Department of Ancient Egypt and Sudan; Department of Greece and Rome. Source material for Rosetta Stone context and Parthenon sculptures.

    www.britishmuseum.org

Scholarly References

  • World History Encyclopedia

    Comprehensive timelines and cultural context articles for Stone Age ritual practices, Roman engineering, and Medieval guild systems.

    www.worldhistory.org
  • Fashion History Timeline

    Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT). In-depth analysis of silhouette evolution from the 18th century (Rococo) through the 1920s (Art Deco).

    fashionhistory.fitnyc.edu
  • Design History Society

    Journal of Design History. Academic articles exploring the socio-economic impacts of the Industrial Revolution vs. Arts & Crafts philosophy.

    www.designhistorysociety.org

Nile

c. 2686 – 2181 BCE

Old Kingdom

The "Age of Pyramids." Establishment of artistic conventions, monumental stone architecture (Giza), and divine kingship.

c. 2055 – 1650 BCE

Middle Kingdom

Classical period of art and literature. Rise of block statues, jewelry sophistication, and deeper cultural unification.

c. 1550 – 1069 BCE

New Kingdom

The Golden Age. Temple complexes (Karnak, Luxor), the Amarna revolution (Akhenaten), and elaborate burial goods (Tutankhamun).

332 BCE – 30 BCE

Ptolemaic & Roman

Greek influence merges with Egyptian tradition. Hybrid deities, Fayum mummy portraits, and the library of Alexandria.

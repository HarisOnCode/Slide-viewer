08. Arts & Crafts Movement

Visual Language:
Nature & Order

Interlacing Florals

Rejecting industrial uniformity, designers embraced complex, organic rhythms found in nature. Patterns featured dense, intertwining foliage, birds, and trellises that maintained a flat, decorative surface rather than illusory depth.

Gothic Revival

Drawing inspiration from medieval craftsmanship, the movement adopted bold outlines, honest construction, and pointed arches. Visuals emphasized structure and utility, where beauty was derived from the quality of execution rather than applied ornament.

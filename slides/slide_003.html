Time

40,000 BCE – 30 BCE

Foundations: Stone Age to Egypt

From primal survival and ritual symbols to monumental permanence and divine order.

800 BCE – 1400 CE

Classical to Medieval

Greek harmony and Roman power give way to faith-driven craftsmanship and feudal structures.

1600 – 1780

The Age of Spectacle

Baroque drama and absolutist power evolve into Rococo's intimate, playful luxury.

1860 – 1910

Reform & Nature

Arts & Crafts rejects industry for honesty; Art Nouveau embraces organic modernity.

1920 – 1939

Modern Geometry

Art Deco celebrates speed, technology, and glamour in the machine age.

Industrial Era

Mass & Speed

Efficiency

Defined by mechanization, standardization, and speed. The focus shifted to mass production, lower costs, and accessibility, often sacrificing material integrity for novelty. Design became separated from making, prioritizing volume over individual craft.

Arts & Crafts

Craft & Honesty

Integrity

A moral reaction emphasizing truth to materials, the joy of labor, and nature-inspired forms. Morris championed ethical production where design and execution were unified. Value lay in the human touch, quality, and the spiritual connection to work.

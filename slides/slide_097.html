Philosophy of Making

Truth to Materials

The movement championed a return to "honest" design, rejecting the deceptive veneers of industrial mass production. Symbols were drawn directly from nature—intertwining flora, local birds, and medieval motifs—celebrating the inherent texture of wood, wool, and paper while making the hand of the craftsman visible in every detail.

L'Art Nouveau

The Whiplash Line

Art Nouveau rejected rigid industrial lines in favor of the organic "whiplash curve"—a dynamic, asymmetrical line inspired by creeping vines and smoke. This philosophy celebrated the Femme Nouvelle, depicting women not as passive figures but as mystical, sensual forces of nature, unified with their surroundings through flowing hair and endless drapery.

Era 05

476 CE – 1000 CE

Early Middle Ages & Romanesque

The fall of Rome leads to monastic preservation of knowledge. Architecture is heavy, fortress-like, with thick walls and rounded arches.

1000 CE – 1300 CE

High Middle Ages & The Gothic Shift

Rise of cities and guilds. Engineering breakthroughs allow for pointed arches, flying buttresses, and walls of stained glass light.

1300 CE – 1500 CE

Late Gothic & Transition

Flamboyant ornamentation and complex tracery. The period ends with social upheaval (Black Death) paving the way for the Renaissance.

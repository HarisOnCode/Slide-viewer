Polytheism & Afterlife

Order, Magic & Eternity

Egyptian design was fundamentally religious, serving a complex pantheon of gods and the journey to the afterlife. Every symbol carried potent magical meaning: the Ankh for life, the Scarab for rebirth, and the Eye of Horus for protection and health. Art was not just decoration, but a tool to ensure eternal existence.

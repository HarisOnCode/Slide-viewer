Baroque Era

Grandeur & Drama

Absolutism

Defined by heavy ornamentation, deep chiaroscuro contrasts, and imposing scale designed to project state and religious power. Garments featured stiff, structured silhouettes with rich velvets and dark jewel tones, emphasizing authority and theatricality.

Rococo Era

Intimacy & Play

Frivolity

Shifted toward asymmetry, pastel palettes, and lighthearted themes of love and nature. Interiors became intimate salons rather than grand halls. Fashion embraced fluidity, floral motifs, and lighter silks, prioritizing pleasure, comfort, and decorative excess.

The Anti-Corset Movement

The Arts & Crafts philosophy extended to the body itself, rejecting the rigid, artificial silhouette of the Victorian corset. "Artistic Dress" championed loose waistlines, soft medieval-inspired drapery, and natural movement. This shift prioritized health, comfort, and the integrity of the natural form over industrial fashion trends.

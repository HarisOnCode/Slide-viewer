The Gold Wreath

Mimicking nature (oak, olive, laurel) in fragile gold sheet, worn for ceremonies and burials rather than daily life.

Filigree Technique

Fine wire work soldered in delicate patterns, creating lightness and detail without heavy metal weight.

Fibula (The Pin)

Essential functional jewelry for securing the chiton and peplos at the shoulders, evolving into decorative brooches.

Signet Rings

Carved gemstones or metal intaglios used for sealing documents and marking identity in civic life.

07. Rococo Era

Rococo Silhouette

Pastels, Paniers & The Art of Artificiality

89

01

The Robe à la Française

Characterized by wide paniers and Watteau pleats at the back. Pastel silks in rose, mint, and sky blue dominated court fashion.

02

Delicate Textiles

Lightweight silks replaced heavy baroque velvets. Floral motifs, ribbons, and lace trimmings created an air of ethereal lightness.

03

Modesty & Allure

The fichu (neckerchief) filled low necklines while corsetry enforced a conical torso shape, emphasizing a narrow waist and exposed décolletage.

04

The Pouf & Headwear

Hairstyles grew vertically, adorned with feathers, pearls, and even model ships. Millinery became a central expressive element of the ensemble.

State Cult & Power

Divinity & Dominion

Rome adapted the Greek pantheon but repurposed it for statecraft. Religion was civic duty, with the Emperor often deified. Key symbols included the Eagle (Jupiter/Empire), the Laurel Wreath (victory/Apollo), and the Fasces (power/unity). These motifs projected authority across vast territories.
